# Workflow Step Template

1. Check RWE design fit.
2. Frame the estimand and question type.
3. choose the best-fit data source and capture structure.
4. Define source population, eligibility, and time zero.
5. Define exposure, comparator, outcomes, and follow-up.
6. Evaluate target-trial-emulation suitability.
7. Review confounding and other major biases.
8. Specify the primary analysis line.
9. Separate verified, assumed, obtainable, and unsupported elements.
10. Give a primary recommendation with safeguards.
