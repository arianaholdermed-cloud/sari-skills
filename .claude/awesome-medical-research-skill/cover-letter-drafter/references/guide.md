# Cover Letter Generation Reference Checklist

## Input Checklist

- Paper title and abstract
- Innovation points and core contributions
- Target journal and submission requirements
- Corresponding author and contact information

## Output Checklist

- Complete cover letter draft
- List of key highlights

## Output Format

- Include salutation, summary description, contribution highlights, and acknowledgments.
- Decide whether to include reviewer suggestions based on journal requirements.

## Quality Check

- Information is consistent with the paper content.
- Highlights contributions and maintains a formal tone.

## Common Issues

- Being too general and failing to highlight innovation points.
- Lacking an explanation of the fit with the journal.
