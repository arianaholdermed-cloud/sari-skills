# Validation Evidence Hierarchy

Order evidence from lower to higher support.

1. Baseline consistency / expression support
2. Perturbation confirmation
3. Primary phenotype effect in one model context
4. Orthogonal support or second-context replication
5. Rescue / specificity support
6. Animal-context support
7. Translational-support linkage back to human evidence

## Escalation Rule
Do not escalate automatically. Escalate only if the lower tier succeeded in a way that makes the next tier informative.

## Go/No-Go Rule
Each stage should define:
- what counts as support,
- what counts as failure,
- what counts as ambiguous and needs redesign.
