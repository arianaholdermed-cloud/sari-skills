# Literature Retrieval and Citation

## Core rule
Never fabricate references.

## If literature is requested
- Prefer real, verifiable primary literature.
- Separate established support from hypothetical or unverified support.
- If exact citations are unavailable in the moment, say that explicit verification is still required.

## Reporting rule
Do not invent PMIDs, DOIs, trial identifiers, model prevalence claims, or “standard model” statements.
