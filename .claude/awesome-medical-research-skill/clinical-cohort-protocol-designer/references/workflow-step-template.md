# Workflow Step Template

Follow this sequence:
1. define the actual cohort question
2. confirm cohort design fit
3. choose retrospective or prospective structure
4. define source population, eligibility, and time-zero
5. define follow-up architecture
6. define endpoints
7. structure variables
8. define the primary analysis line
9. review bias and validity
10. review feasibility and data quality
11. recommend the lead protocol version

Do not skip time-zero, endpoint definition, or analysis-line selection.
