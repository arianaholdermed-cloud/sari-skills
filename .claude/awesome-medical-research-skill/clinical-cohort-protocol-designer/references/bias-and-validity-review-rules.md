# Bias and Validity Review Rules

Every cohort protocol should explicitly review the main validity threats.

Common threats:
- selection bias
- confounding by indication
- immortal time bias
- exposure or endpoint misclassification
- informative censoring
- missing covariates
- center-level heterogeneity
- limited event counts
- residual confounding

Required behavior:
- For each major bias threat, state why it matters and how the protocol tries to mitigate it.
- Also state where mitigation is incomplete.
