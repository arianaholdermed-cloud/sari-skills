# Enrollment and Eligibility Framework

Define the cohort from the source population outward.

Minimum structure:
- source population / sampling frame
- inclusion criteria
- exclusion criteria
- cohort entry rule
- handling of repeated admissions, repeated treatments, or multiple qualifying episodes

Important distinction:
- Eligibility criteria determine who enters the cohort.
- Analysis subgroup criteria determine where prespecified heterogeneity or effect modification will be examined.

Do not hide key design decisions inside vague phrases like “eligible patients” or “appropriate cases.”
