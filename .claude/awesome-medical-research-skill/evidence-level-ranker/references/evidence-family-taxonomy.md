Classify papers into explicit evidence families rather than using vague labels.

Common families include:
- evidence synthesis: systematic review, meta-analysis, umbrella review, scoping review
- interventional: randomized trial, non-randomized intervention, pragmatic trial
- observational clinical: cohort, case-control, cross-sectional, registry analysis, real-world evidence
- predictive / prognostic modeling: signature studies, risk score development, validation studies
- translational biomarker: assay-linked clinical correlation, diagnostic or prognostic biomarker studies
- mechanism / experimental: cell, animal, perturbation, pathway mechanism studies
- omics / discovery: transcriptomics, proteomics, single-cell, spatial, multi-omics discovery papers
- validation / replication: external validation, orthogonal validation, independent replication

Always separate:
- design label
- evidence family
- main inferential purpose
