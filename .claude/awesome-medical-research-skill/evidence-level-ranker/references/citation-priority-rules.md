Assign citation priority based on what role the paper should play in scientific writing.

Recommended categories:
- anchor citation: should carry major argumentative weight
- strong supporting citation: supports an important but not singular claim
- context-setting citation: useful for field positioning or background
- exploratory citation: useful for hypothesis generation or niche points
- low-priority citation: should not anchor core claims

A paper may be important historically or conceptually while still being a low-priority anchor citation.
