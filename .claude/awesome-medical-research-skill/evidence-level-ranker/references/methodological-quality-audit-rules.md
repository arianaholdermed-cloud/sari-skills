Audit execution quality, not just design labels.

Check as relevant:
- sample adequacy for the claim made
- confounding control
- endpoint relevance and clarity
- missingness handling
- model leakage or overfitting risk
- calibration, not only discrimination, for predictive models
- comparator quality
- measurement reproducibility
- protocol transparency
- sensitivity analysis or robustness checks

A nominally weaker design may outrank a stronger design if the stronger design is poorly executed.
