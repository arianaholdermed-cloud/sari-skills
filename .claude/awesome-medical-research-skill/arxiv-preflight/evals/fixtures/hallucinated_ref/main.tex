\begin{abstract}
We study sparse routing strategies in mixture-of-experts (MoE) transformers and
report a 1.8x throughput improvement over dense baselines on the C4 corpus.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction}
Mixture-of-experts has been widely studied~\cite{shazeer2017outrageously,
fedus2022switch}. Prior work on quantum-routed gating mechanisms reported
strong gains \cite{liang2023quantum}.

\section{Method}
We follow the routing formulation of \cite{lepikhin2021gshard} with a learned
sparsity loss inspired by \cite{nguyen2024recursive}.

\section{Results}
On the C4 corpus our 8-expert model achieves a 1.8x throughput improvement at
equal validation loss compared to a dense Transformer.
