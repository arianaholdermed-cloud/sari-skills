# Workflow Step Template

1. Restate the target contrast.
2. Clarify time order.
3. Classify variable roles.
4. Identify plausible confounders.
5. Select the minimum defensible control set.
6. Choose the best-fit control strategy.
7. Review bias risks with strong sensing.
8. Surface weak points and residual uncertainty.
9. State the most important immediate protocol revision.
