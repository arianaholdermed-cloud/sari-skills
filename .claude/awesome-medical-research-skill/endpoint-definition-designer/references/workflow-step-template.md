# Workflow Step Template

1. Decide whether clarification is mandatory.
2. Restate the study objective and endpoint role.
3. Choose and stress-test the primary endpoint.
4. Define secondary and exploratory endpoints.
5. Specify event, timing, and composite rules.
6. Operationalize endpoint capture.
7. Review ambiguity, interpretability, and feasibility risks.
8. Produce the structured endpoint framework.
