# Narrative Selection Rules

## Core Rule
Only narrate the details that are most useful for reader understanding.

## Good Candidates For Prose
- major group differences,
- primary model estimates,
- especially strong or clinically relevant contrasts,
- summary pattern across a table,
- results that connect directly to the manuscript’s main claim.

## Leave In The Table
- repetitive rows,
- low-value detail,
- secondary numeric granularity,
- mechanically repeated confidence intervals unless central.

## Reporting Rule
The narrative should feel selective and purposeful, not exhaustive.
