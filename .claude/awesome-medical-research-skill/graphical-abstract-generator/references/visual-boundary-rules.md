# Visual Boundary Rules

## Core Rule
Graphical simplification must not increase the evidentiary claim of the study.

## Keep Separate
- association vs mechanism,
- workflow vs result,
- support vs proof,
- implication vs implementation readiness.

## Important Rule
A cleaner picture must not become a stronger claim than the paper supports.

## Reporting Rule
State what the graphical abstract may communicate and what it still must not imply.
