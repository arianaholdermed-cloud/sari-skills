## Evidence Tiers

### Instrument and Primary Estimate Evidence (supportive — label explicitly as causal-prioritization evidence)

| Evidence | Source | What It Establishes |
|---|---|---|
| Strong instruments | SNP threshold + F-statistic + clumping | usable instrument basis |
| Harmonized exposure-outcome pairs | allele alignment and QC | valid analysis-ready inputs |
| IVW estimate | primary MR analysis | main causal-prioritization result |

### Sensitivity and Robustness Evidence (stronger — label explicitly as robustness support)

| Evidence | Source | What It Establishes |
|---|---|---|
| Complementary estimators | weighted median / MR-Egger / mode | estimator coherence |
| Heterogeneity review | Cochran-style metrics or equivalent | stability of instrument set |
| Pleiotropy review | intercept / outlier logic | reduced concern about directional pleiotropy |
| Leave-one-out review | sequential SNP omission | lower risk of single-SNP domination |

### Extended Causal Architecture Evidence (supportive — label explicitly as extension evidence)

| Evidence | Source | What It Establishes |
|---|---|---|
| Reverse MR | directionality challenge test | one alternative-direction check |
| MVMR | correlated-exposure adjustment | conditional causal support |
| Steiger / orientation support | directionality plausibility | stronger orientation argument |

### Replication and Triangulation Evidence (stronger — label explicitly as validation support)

| Evidence | Source | What It Establishes |
|---|---|---|
| Independent GWAS replication | second outcome or exposure GWAS | direction-consistent support |
| Orthogonal triangulation | external epidemiologic or biological context | contextual coherence |
| Conservative multi-layer agreement | primary + sensitivity + extension + replication alignment | stronger prioritization credibility |

---

## Language Rules

- Primary findings: "supports a causal association", "genetically proxied evidence suggests"
- Robustness findings: "directionally consistent across estimators", "less sensitive to single-SNP influence"
- Extension findings: "supports conditional association", "provides reverse-direction challenge"
- Validation findings: "replication support", "triangulation support"

- **NEVER use:** "proves causality", "confirms intervention effect", "establishes treatment benefit" from two-sample MR alone
