# Design and Bias-Control Rules

Use this module when judging whether a field is methodologically limited by study design or bias control.

## Review Questions
- Is the comparator appropriate?
- Is temporality aligned to the claim?
- Is confounding handled adequately for the claim being made?
- Are inclusion/exclusion criteria likely to distort generalizability?
- Is selection bias likely?
- Are endpoint definitions consistent and decision-relevant?

## Important Rule
Do not call a study design “strong” only because it is large or retrospective. Cohort size does not remove design bias.
