# Results Boundary Rules

## Core Rule
The Results section should present findings clearly without drifting into Discussion-style interpretation.

## Keep Separate
- observed findings,
- supporting analyses,
- validation evidence,
- mechanistic plausibility,
- speculative interpretation.

## Important Rule
A writing improvement must not change the evidence level of the study.

## Reporting Rule
State what the Results draft may support and what it still must not imply.
