# Paragraph Role Control Rules

## Core Rule
Each paragraph must perform a clear narrative role.

## Common Roles
- Paragraph 1: define the problem and its relevance
- Paragraph 2: summarize what is known and where current understanding/practice is insufficient
- Paragraph 3: sharpen the specific gap
- Final paragraph: position the current study and state the objective

## Important Rule
Do not let multiple paragraphs repeat the same background function.
Paragraph role duplication weakens the Introduction.
