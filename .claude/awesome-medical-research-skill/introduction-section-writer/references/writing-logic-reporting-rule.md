# Writing Logic Reporting Rule

## Core Rule
Every meaningful writing choice should be explained.

## Explain In Terms Of
- sharper opening problem framing,
- tighter background selection,
- clearer gap narrowing,
- more accurate study positioning,
- reduced literature-dump behavior,
- better paragraph transition control.

## Important Rule
Do not say only that the prose is “better.”
State why the change improves scientific readability and positioning.
