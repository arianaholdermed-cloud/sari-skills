# Hybrid Study Boundary Rules

## Core Rule
Not every manuscript fits neatly into a single reporting-guideline family.

## Typical Hybrid Situations
- observational studies with prediction-model components
- biomarker studies with validation layers
- mixed clinical + omics manuscripts
- evidence synthesis papers with modeling add-ons

## Important Rule
Do not overclaim formal compliance with one framework when the paper has meaningful reporting needs from another.

## Reporting Rule
State when the manuscript has mixed reporting expectations and explain how that affects the review.
