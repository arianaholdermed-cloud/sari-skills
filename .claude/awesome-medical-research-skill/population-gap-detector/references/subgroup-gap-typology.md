# Subgroup Gap Typology

Use this module to classify what kind of population gap is actually present.

## Primary Gap Types
1. Missing population
2. Underrepresented population
3. Pooled-but-unresolved subgroup
4. Inconsistent subgroup finding
5. Subgroup without independent validation
6. Biologically plausible subgroup with weak evidence depth
7. Clinically important subgroup with weak study targeting

## Rule
Name the gap type explicitly. Do not use vague language such as “there may be a subgroup issue” when a more specific classification is possible.
