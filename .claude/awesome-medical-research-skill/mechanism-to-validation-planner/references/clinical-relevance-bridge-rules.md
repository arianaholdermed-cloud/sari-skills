# Clinical Relevance Bridge Rules

Use this module when deciding whether and how the validation pathway should extend toward patient-level relevance.

## Appropriate Clinical-Relevance Bridges
- patient-level correlation after stronger biological support
- biomarker-context linkage when the finding can plausibly be measured and interpreted
- subgroup or outcome relevance when the biological claim has enough stability
- translational framing when the use-case is explicit and bounded

## Caution Conditions
Do not force a clinical extension when:
- the biology is still too descriptive
- the mechanism is too weakly anchored
- the measurement route is unclear
- the implied use-case is not yet defined

## Reporting Rule
State whether the clinical bridge is:
- appropriate now
- appropriate later
- premature at the current stage
