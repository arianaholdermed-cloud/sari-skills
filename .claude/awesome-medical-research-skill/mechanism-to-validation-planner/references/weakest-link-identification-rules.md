# Weakest Link Identification Rules

Use this module to identify the most important bottleneck in the current evidence chain.

## Common Weakest Links
- unstable association base
- no orthogonal confirmation
- no perturbation support
- no mechanism specificity
- no context robustness
- no patient-level or use-case relevance
- strong wording with thin evidence

## Reporting Rule
State:
- the weakest link
- why it matters
- what step would most directly address it
- what overclaim risk remains if it is ignored
