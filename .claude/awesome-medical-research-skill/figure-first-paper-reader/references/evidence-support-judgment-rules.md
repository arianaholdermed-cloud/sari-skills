Support judgments:
- Strong support: the visual evidence directly and clearly supports the attached claim.
- Partial support: the figure supports only part of the claim or does so with notable limits.
- Weak support: the figure is suggestive but insufficient for the claim strength used in the narrative.
- Does not establish the claim on its own: the claim requires missing analyses, methods, validation, or additional evidence.

Judge support from the visual evidence actually available.
