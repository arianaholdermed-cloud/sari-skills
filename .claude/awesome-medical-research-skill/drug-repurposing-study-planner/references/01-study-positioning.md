# Study Positioning Rules

## Core Rule
This skill designs a **drug repurposing study framework**. It does not write a full protocol and does not recommend treatment to patients.

## Important Distinction
The output must stay at the level of:
- route selection,
- evidence architecture,
- prioritization logic,
- and validation planning.

Do not drift into:
- final drug recommendation,
- dosing advice,
- trial operations,
- or unsupported therapeutic claims.
