# Repurposing Route Selection Rules

## Primary Route Rule
Always choose one primary route family.

Possible route families include:
- target/mechanism-led,
- expression-reversal-led,
- genetics-supported,
- RWE-supported,
- translational convergence,
- or a staged hybrid with one clear lead route.

## Comparison Rule
Alternative routes may be acknowledged, but they must not be presented as equally central unless the user explicitly asks for a route comparison exercise.

## Reporting Rule
State:
- why the chosen route is primary,
- what it can realistically establish,
- and what it cannot establish on its own.
