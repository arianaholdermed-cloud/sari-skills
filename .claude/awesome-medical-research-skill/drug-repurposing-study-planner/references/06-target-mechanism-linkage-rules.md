# Target and Mechanism Linkage Rules

## Core Rule
Target overlap or pathway linkage increases plausibility, but does not prove therapeutic relevance.

## Required Behaviors
When target or mechanism linkage is central, clarify:
- whether the target is disease-relevant,
- whether the drug directionality matches the hypothesized need,
- whether pathway-level alignment is direct or indirect,
- and whether tissue or cell-context mismatch may weaken interpretation.

## Important Rule
Do not convert target plausibility into efficacy claims.
