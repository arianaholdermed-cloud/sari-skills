# Disease-Side and Drug-Side Framing Rules

## Disease-Side Inputs
Possible disease-side anchors include:
- phenotype,
- subtype,
- pathway,
- target,
- signature,
- clinical unmet need,
- or genetically supported biology.

## Drug-Side Inputs
Possible drug-side anchors include:
- known targets,
- pathway modulation profile,
- perturbation signature,
- approved indication context,
- route of administration,
- safety or translational plausibility.

## Framing Rule
Explicitly state whether the study starts from:
- disease biology and moves toward candidate drugs,
- drug class and moves toward indication relevance,
- or a convergence strategy using both.
