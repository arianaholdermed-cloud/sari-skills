# RWE and Clinical Support Rules

## Core Rule
RWE can strengthen prioritization or translational plausibility, but observational support does not equal therapeutic proof.

## Use Cases
RWE may support:
- prescribing-outcome association,
- adverse event context,
- treatment patterns,
- subgroup plausibility,
- or external relevance.

## Important Rule
State clearly whether RWE is used for:
- signal support,
- triangulation,
- or a dedicated downstream study.

Do not present observational findings as causal treatment effect without appropriate design and caveats.
