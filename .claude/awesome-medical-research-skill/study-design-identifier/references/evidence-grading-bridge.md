# Evidence Grading Bridge

This module links study design labels to downstream literature appraisal.

## Evidence-family grouping
- Interventional evidence
- Observational evidence
- Diagnostic / prognostic evidence
- Computational / omics evidence
- Experimental / mechanistic evidence
- Synthesis-level evidence
- Hybrid evidence chain

## Use rule
This module helps place the paper in an appraisal framework, but it does not by itself assign evidence strength. Do not equate design family with final credibility.
