# Study Design Taxonomy

Use this taxonomy to classify papers by actual design structure rather than author wording.

## Core clinical and epidemiologic designs
- Randomized controlled trial
- Non-randomized interventional study
- Prospective cohort study
- Retrospective cohort study
- Case-control study
- Cross-sectional study
- Registry or database analysis
- Real-world evidence study
- Diagnostic accuracy study
- Prognostic or prediction-model study
- Systematic review / meta-analysis

## Core biomedical and translational designs
- Omics screening study
- Biomarker discovery study
- Biomarker validation study
- Mechanism experiment
- Target-validation experiment
- Animal model study
- Cell-based perturbation study
- Hybrid translational study

## Key design tests
Ask:
1. Was there intervention allocation?
2. Was allocation randomized?
3. Were participants grouped by exposure, by outcome, or by a single-timepoint sample?
4. Was follow-up longitudinal or not?
5. Is the core evidence clinical, observational, computational, or experimental?
6. Is the study mainly exploratory, mainly validating, or truly hybrid?
