# Druggability and Modality Framework

Assess:
- tractability of the target class
- intracellular vs extracellular accessibility
- enzymatic vs scaffolding vs transcriptional behavior
- modality fit: small molecule, antibody, ADC, degrader, RNA, gene/cell therapy
- biomarker/patient-selection logic
- likely safety and selectivity constraints
- whether indirect pathway intervention is more plausible than direct targeting
