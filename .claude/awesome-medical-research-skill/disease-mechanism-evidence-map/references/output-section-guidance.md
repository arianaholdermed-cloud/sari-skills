# Output Section Guidance

The final output should remain structured and layer-aware.

Recommended sections:
- disease scope definition,
- major mechanism axes,
- layered mechanism chain map,
- cell and tissue context map,
- phenotype link map,
- key evidence chain table,
- evidence strength and chain completeness,
- weak links and mechanistic uncertainty,
- mechanism hypothesis entry points,
- suggested next step,
- verified supporting literature when citations are included.

If formal literature citations are included, keep them real, verifiable, and explicit about any missing DOI or incomplete verification.
