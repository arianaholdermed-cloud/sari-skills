# Literature Verification and Citation Rules

When formal literature citations are included:
- cite only real, verifiable papers,
- include DOI whenever available,
- include a stable link,
- keep source identity transparent.

Minimum formal citation fields:
- title,
- first author,
- year,
- journal or venue,
- DOI when available,
- stable link.

If DOI is unavailable, say `DOI not available`.
If DOI exists but cannot be verified, say `DOI not verified`.
If the paper itself cannot be verified, do not present it as formal supporting evidence.

Never fabricate:
- title,
- author list,
- year,
- journal,
- DOI,
- PMID,
- link.
