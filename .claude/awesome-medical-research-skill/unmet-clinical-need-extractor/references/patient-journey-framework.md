# Patient Journey Framework

Use this module to locate unmet needs across the clinical pathway.

A clinically useful unmet-need extraction should show where along the patient journey current care underperforms, not just state that better care is needed.

## Common Patient-Journey Stages

- risk identification or early screening
- initial diagnostic workup
- molecular or clinical stratification
- treatment selection
- treatment-response prediction
- on-treatment monitoring
- relapse detection or residual disease monitoring
- progression or resistance management
- toxicity balancing and de-escalation decisions
- survivorship or long-term follow-up
- access, implementation, or workflow adoption

## Important Rules

- Map unmet need to a concrete stage whenever possible.
- Do not collapse the whole disease pathway into one generic pain point.
- Distinguish biological uncertainty from clinical workflow failure.
- Distinguish unmet need in ideal-trial settings from unmet need in routine care.
