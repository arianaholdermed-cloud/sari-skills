# Output Section Guidance

## Section A
Interpret the real question, not just the topic label.

## Section B
List only serious candidate route families plus obvious weak surface options if relevant.

## Section C
Make route-to-question alignment explicit.

## Section D
Remove weak options early instead of comparing everything equally.

## Section E
Use a structured comparison matrix when side-by-side comparison helps.

## Section F
Recommend one primary plan decisively.

## Section G
Explain why the recommended route should lead.

## Section H
State clearly why other routes should not be primary.

## Section I
Make dependency assumptions visible.

## Section J
Always recommend a downstream next step.
