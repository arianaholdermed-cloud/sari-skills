# Read / Skim / Skip Rules

## Core Rule
Convert the screening judgment into a practical reading action.

## Full Read
Use when the paper is strongly relevant, methodologically useful, or strategically important.

## Skim
Use when the paper is somewhat relevant, broadly informative, or useful for limited reasons but not worth deep time investment.

## Skip
Use when the paper is weakly relevant, low-yield, or unlikely to change the user's thinking or action.

## Uncertain Pending Fuller Text
Use when the available input is too thin for a confident triage decision.

## Reporting Rule
State clearly why the paper falls into the chosen bucket.
