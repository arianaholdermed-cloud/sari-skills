# Workflow Step Template

1. Decide whether clarification is required before long output.
2. Restate the target population and likely study context.
3. Define the source population and index/screening frame.
4. Draft inclusion criteria.
5. Draft exclusion criteria.
6. Add timing anchors and operational evidence sources.
7. Pressure-test ambiguity, contradiction, and overrestriction.
8. List adjudication needs and unresolved clarifications.
9. Label the draft status honestly.
