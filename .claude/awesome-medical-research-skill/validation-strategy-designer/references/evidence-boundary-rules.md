# Evidence Boundary Rules

## Core Rule
Keep validation evidence layers separate.

## Important Distinctions
- Cross-validation does not equal independent replication.
- Temporal validation does not automatically prove broad transportability.
- Technical replication does not equal biological mechanism validation.
- Functional support does not automatically prove clinical utility.
- External association support does not equal implementation readiness.

## Reporting Rule
Whenever you describe validation strength, specify what kind of validation has been achieved or planned.
Avoid blanket language such as “fully validated.”
