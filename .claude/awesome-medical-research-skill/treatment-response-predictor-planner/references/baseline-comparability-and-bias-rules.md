# Baseline Comparability and Bias Rules

## Core Rule
Predictive interpretation depends on baseline comparability, treatment-selection structure, and control of treatment-context heterogeneity.

## Must Review
- channeling or treatment-selection bias
- pooled-regimen heterogeneity
- therapy-line imbalance
- disease burden imbalance at baseline
- prior treatment exposure imbalance
- interpretation drift from prediction to causal effect claims

## Important Rule
If baseline imbalance is severe and poorly characterized, the study may still support prediction, but its interpretation limits must be made explicit.
