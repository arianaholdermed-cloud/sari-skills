# Measurability and Executability Rules

After rewriting, assess whether the objective is ready for downstream study work.

## Measurable
Can the objective be linked to defined variables, readouts, comparisons, or assessments?

## Executable
Can a plausible design, cohort, experiment, or analysis plan be built from the objective?

## Appropriately Bounded
Is the objective narrow enough to prevent major downstream ambiguity?

## Typical Outcomes
- objective is ready for aim setting and study design
- objective is improved but still missing key operational elements
- objective remains too broad and needs further narrowing

## Important Distinction
A refined objective can sound precise but still remain non-executable.
