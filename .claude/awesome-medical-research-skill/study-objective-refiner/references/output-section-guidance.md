# Output Section Guidance

## Section A
Interpret the study intention rather than merely restating the original sentence.

## Section B
Classify the objective type explicitly and name important secondary types.

## Section C
Expose what is missing operationally; do not hide it.

## Section D
State the confirmatory versus exploratory posture clearly.

## Section E
Use a structured breakdown only when it materially improves clarity.

## Section F
Provide three levels of refinement: plain-language, protocol-ready, downstream-design-ready.

## Section G
State both what is covered and what is intentionally excluded.

## Section H
Be honest about whether the objective is now measurable and executable.

## Section I
Always recommend a next-step path.

## Section J
Warn the user how the objective is still most likely to be misframed.
