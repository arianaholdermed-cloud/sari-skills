# Ambiguity and Scope Rules

Use this module to identify where the current objective is too vague, too broad, or too mixed.

## Common Ambiguity Signals
- undefined population
- undefined disease stage or context
- undefined endpoint or readout
- no comparison context where one is needed
- broad verbs with no operational meaning
- multiple hidden aims inside one sentence

## Scope Drift Signals
- discovery and validation merged without boundary
- biology and clinical utility merged without transition logic
- too many outcomes embedded in a single objective
- broad disease framing with no practical limits

## Rule
Make ambiguity visible. Do not hide it behind polished wording.
