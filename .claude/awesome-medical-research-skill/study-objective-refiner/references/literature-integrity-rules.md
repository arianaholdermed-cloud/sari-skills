# Literature Integrity Rules

Use this module whenever prior studies, established findings, precedent claims, or literature-backed wording are mentioned.

## Never Fabricate
Do not fabricate:
- references
- PMIDs
- DOIs
- trial names
- prior findings
- validation status
- precedent claims

## Rule
If literature support cannot be verified, label it explicitly as limited, unverified, or evidence-thin.

## Important Distinction
Polished objective wording must not imply stronger evidence support than the literature can justify.
