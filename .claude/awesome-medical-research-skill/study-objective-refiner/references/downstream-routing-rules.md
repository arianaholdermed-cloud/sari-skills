# Downstream Routing Rules

After refining the objective, recommend the best next-step tool or workflow.

## Route to Aim and Hypothesis Designer
Use when the objective is now bounded enough to define primary and secondary aims and formulate testable hypotheses.

## Route to Study Planner / Protocol Framing
Use when the objective is already concrete enough to support design selection and workflow planning.

## Route to Literature / Gap Workflow
Use when the objective is improved but still depends on unresolved evidence positioning or gap confirmation.

## Route to Method Review
Use when the objective is clear but the user needs to understand the design, analysis, or validation upgrades required.

## Rule
Do not leave the user with a refined objective but no recommended next step.
