# Minimum Viable Study Rules

Use this module to define the smallest study version that still produces interpretable value.

## Preserve These First
- the core question
- the main comparison or signal of interest
- the minimum evidence required for interpretability

## Cut These First
- decorative complexity
- weakly justified add-on analyses
- fragile validation wishes
- premature mechanism layers
- optional expansion modules

## Important Rule
Minimum viable does not mean trivial. It means the study can realistically finish and still support a coherent conclusion.
