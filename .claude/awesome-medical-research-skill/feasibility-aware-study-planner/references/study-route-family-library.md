# Study-Route Family Library

Use this module to identify the broad study route that best matches the question under the real constraint profile.

## Common Route Families
- retrospective clinical cohort
- public-data bioinformatics study
- translational biomarker study
- mechanism-first validation study
- real-world evidence study
- staged mixed-route study with one clear lead component

## Important Rule
Do not recommend a hybrid route unless one component clearly leads and the others are genuinely support layers.

## Reporting Rule
Name the lead route family explicitly.
