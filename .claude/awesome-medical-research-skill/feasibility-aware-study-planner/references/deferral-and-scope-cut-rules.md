# Deferral and Scope-Cut Rules

Use this module when deciding what should be postponed, removed, or converted into future-stage work.

## Common Deferral Targets
- external validation
- wet-lab mechanism confirmation
- multi-center expansion
- longitudinal extension
- heavy subgroup exploration
- complex model comparison layers

## Important Rule
Deferred work should remain logically connected to the primary executable version. Do not defer random attractive extras without naming why.

## Reporting Rule
State what was cut, why it was cut, and what would be needed to restore it later.
