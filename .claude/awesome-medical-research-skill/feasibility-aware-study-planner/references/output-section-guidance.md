# Output Section Guidance

## Section A
State the real study goal rather than repeating the user’s broad wording.

## Section B
Rank constraints instead of listing them as if they are equal.

## Section C
Make the resource boundary explicit: available, obtainable, unavailable.

## Section D
Name the candidate route family cleanly.

## Section E
Tie every major design change to a real constraint.

## Section F
Define the minimum executable version without turning it into a weak or incoherent study.

## Section G
Surface critical failure points rather than generic limitations.

## Section H
Be honest about what was deliberately deferred or removed.

## Section I
Give one lead recommendation.

## Section J
Make assumption dependence visible.
