# Tone and Boundary Rules

## Core Rule
The response should be professional, respectful, and bounded.

## Good Tone Characteristics
- acknowledges the reviewer concern
- answers the issue directly
- avoids defensiveness
- avoids empty apology language
- avoids overclaiming what was fixed

## Important Rule
Professional tone does not mean surrendering every scientific point.

## Reporting Rule
Where disagreement is needed, keep it evidence-based and proportionate.
