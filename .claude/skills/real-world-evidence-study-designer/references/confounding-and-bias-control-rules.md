# Confounding and Bias Control Rules

## Core Rule
Bias review is mandatory in RWE design.

## Must Review When Relevant
- confounding by indication
- disease severity confounding
- channeling bias
- immortal time bias
- informative censoring
- exposure misclassification
- outcome misclassification
- missing-data-related selection issues
- linkage bias or health-system capture bias

## Important Rule
Do not imply that propensity scores, weighting, or advanced models automatically solve unmeasured confounding.

## Reporting Rule
State the most likely strongest bias, the most assumption-dependent design element, and the easiest result to over-interpret.
