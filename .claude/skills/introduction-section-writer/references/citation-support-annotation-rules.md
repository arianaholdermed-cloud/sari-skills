# Citation Support Annotation Rules

## Core Rule
When a sentence or claim clearly needs literature support, explicitly mark:
the required citation-support marker

Then provide a corresponding PubMed search query.

## Common Citation-Needing Statements
- disease burden or clinical importance statements,
- claims about current standard practice,
- statements about prior research trends,
- claims about unresolved limitations,
- statements about why a specific gap matters.

## Important Rule
Do not fabricate actual references, PMIDs, or DOIs.
Mark the need for citation support and provide a search query instead.

## Opt-Out Rule
If the user explicitly says they do not want citation-support annotation, omit it.
