# Study Positioning and Claim Boundary Rules

## Core Rule
Full prose must preserve the paper’s real contribution boundary.

## Do
- position the study around its actual design and evidence type,
- present the study as addressing a specific limitation,
- keep objective language aligned with what the study can support.

## Do Not
- imply definitive mechanism without support,
- imply clinical readiness without validation,
- imply causal certainty from associative or exploratory evidence,
- oversell exploratory work as resolution of a major unmet need.

## Important Rule
Elegant prose does not justify stronger claims.
