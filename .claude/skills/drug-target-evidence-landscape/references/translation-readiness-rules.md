# Translation Readiness Rules

Judge readiness by:
- strength of disease linkage
- tractability
- modality plausibility
- translational biomarkers
- preclinical robustness
- clinical signal strength
- safety feasibility
- positioning clarity
- competitive differentiation

Common blockers:
- biologic redundancy
- poor tractability
- toxicity risk
- weak patient selection
- shallow clinical effect
- undifferentiated crowding
