# Next-Step Structuring Rules

## Core Rule
Next-step slides should turn open problems into concrete discussion items without pretending the plan is already final.

## Good Next-Step Slides Usually Include
- the main unresolved question
- the most plausible next analysis or experiment
- the dependency or blocker
- the decision the meeting should help resolve

## Important Rule
A vague “future work” slide is usually weak for lab meetings.

## Reporting Rule
Make the next-step section discussion-ready, not decorative.
