# Translation Linkage Rules

Use this module to connect unmet clinical needs to research-value framing.

A useful unmet-need extraction should help the user frame why a biomarker, model, assay, mechanism, or translational study matters clinically.

## Common Research-Value Linkages

- screening or early-detection development
- diagnostic clarification
- subtype or risk stratification
- prognosis when prognosis changes management relevance
- treatment-response prediction
- monitoring or MRD-related development
- target/pathway work tied to a defined clinical bottleneck
- implementation-oriented optimization

## Important Rules

- Do not claim a translational path unless the clinical need naturally supports it.
- Do not move from “important disease” to “therefore any biomarker is valuable.”
- Keep the research-value framing no broader than the extracted need supports.
- Separate clinically grounded framing from aspirational downstream possibilities.
