# Output Section Guidance

## Section A
Restate the study and label assumptions clearly.

## Section B
Define the source population and screening frame in practical terms.

## Sections C and D
Use numbered inclusion and exclusion criteria. Keep each criterion single-purpose when possible.

## Section E
State timing anchors explicitly.

## Section F
Use a table when operational evidence mapping matters.

## Sections G and H
Be critical. Surface ambiguity, contradiction, reviewer-disagreement risk, and likely screening burden.

## Section I
List the minimal unresolved questions, not a long wish list.

## Section J
Give a clear draft-readiness status.
