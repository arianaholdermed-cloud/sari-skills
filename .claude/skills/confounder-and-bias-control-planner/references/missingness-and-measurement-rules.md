# Missingness and Measurement Rules

## Core Rule
Confounder control depends on how variables are measured, when they are measured, and whether missingness is balanced.

## Review Points
- timing of measurement
- ascertainment symmetry across groups
- completeness
- likely differential missingness
- proxy quality
- coding fidelity

## Important Rule
A confounder that is poorly measured may not adequately control confounding and may create false reassurance.

## Reporting Rule
Flag the variables whose measurement quality most threatens validity and recommend practical mitigation steps.
