# Strategy Selection Rules

## Core Rule
Choose the simplest defensible control strategy that fits the target contrast, design, and covariate structure.

## Candidate Strategies
- restriction
- matching
- stratification
- multivariable adjustment
- propensity score weighting or matching
- standardization
- negative controls
- layered strategy

## Important Rule
Do not recommend advanced methods by default. Justify them from the design problem.

## Reporting Rule
State why the preferred strategy is the best first-line choice and what assumptions it relies on.
