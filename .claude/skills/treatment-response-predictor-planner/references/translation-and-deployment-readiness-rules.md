# Translation and Deployment Readiness Rules

## Core Rule
A predictor is not translation-ready just because it shows internal discrimination.

## Must Review
- baseline availability before treatment decision
- assay or modality turnaround
- platform standardization and reproducibility
- feasibility in the intended care pathway
- whether external validation and threshold stability are still missing

## Important Rule
If the predictor cannot be obtained in the real treatment decision window, translation readiness should be downgraded.
