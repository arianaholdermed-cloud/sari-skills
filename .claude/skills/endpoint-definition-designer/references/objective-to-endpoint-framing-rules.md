# Objective-to-Endpoint Framing Rules

Translate the study objective into endpoint role.
- descriptive objective → descriptive or incidence-oriented endpoint framing
- prognostic objective → time-to-event or risk-oriented endpoint framing
- treatment response objective → response, progression, resistance, or clinically anchored benefit framing
- translational objective → clinically meaningful or mechanistically interpretable endpoint framing

Core Rule:
The endpoint hierarchy must follow the study objective. Do not select endpoints first and justify them later.
