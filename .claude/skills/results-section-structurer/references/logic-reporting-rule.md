# Logic Reporting Rule

## Core Rule
Every major structuring choice should be explained.

## Explain In Terms Of
- clearer primary-result visibility,
- better setup-to-result transition,
- reduced fragmentation,
- improved grouping of support analyses,
- cleaner placement of validation,
- protection against overinterpretation.

## Important Rule
Do not say only that the Results are “clearer.”
State why the order improves scientific readability.
