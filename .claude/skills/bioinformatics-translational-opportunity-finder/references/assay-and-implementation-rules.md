# Assay and Implementation Rules

Use this module to assess whether a bioinformatics finding could plausibly move beyond discovery.

Review the following:
- specimen accessibility
- assay practicality
- transferability from the discovery platform to a usable assay
- cross-lab or cross-cohort reproducibility expectations
- interpretability of the output
- turnaround burden
- cost and operational complexity
- plausible position in an actual workflow

Important rule:
A biologically interesting signature is not automatically assayable, scalable, or workflow-compatible.
