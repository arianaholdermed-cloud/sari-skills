# Research Stream Clustering Rules

## Purpose
Group the literature into recognizable streams rather than discussing isolated studies only.

## Typical Stream Categories
Possible stream families include:
- mechanism-focused,
- biomarker / prognosis,
- intervention / treatment,
- omics profiling,
- translational validation,
- algorithm / method-centered,
- clinical outcome / cohort studies.

## Rule
Each major stream should answer:
- what question it usually asks,
- what material or population it usually studies,
- what methods it commonly uses.
