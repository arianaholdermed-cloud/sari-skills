# Output Section Guidance

## Required Sections
A valid output should include:
- Topic Scope Definition
- Evidence Mapping Frame
- Major Research Streams
- Population and Setting Map
- Endpoint and Outcome Map
- Method Map
- Evidence Density and Saturation
- Thin Areas and Weak Spots
- Entry-Point Suggestions
- Suggested Next Step

## Style Rule
The output should read like a field map, not like a full review article.

## Caution Rule
Thin areas must not be promoted as validated gaps unless a dedicated gap-analysis step is performed afterward.
